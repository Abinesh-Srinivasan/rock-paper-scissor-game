About:
Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors! Rock, Paper, Scissors is a classic hand game that's fun, simple, and always a great way to make quick decisions or settle disputes. In this game, you'll face off against the computer in a battle of wits. Will you choose the mighty Rock, the versatile Paper, or the sharp Scissors? It's a game of chance and strategy, where each choice can lead to victory or Lose or a Tie. Can you outsmart the computer and become the Rock, Paper, Scissors champion? Click the buttons below to make your move and find out! Have fun and may the best hand win!
Note: Strong Player will get 50 wins within 1 minute.
